Looking at the code, the issue is in the `Email.__call__` method. When an IDN domain is encoded via `domain_part.encode("idna").decode("ascii")`, the resulting punycode is then validated against `DOMAIN_REGEX`. However, the `encode("idna")` call doesn't raise an error for labels starting or ending with hyphens - it encodes them, and the resulting punycode might pass the regex.

The fix is to validate each label of the domain to ensure none start or end with a hyphen before accepting the IDN-encoded form.
