Looking at the code, the issue is in the `Email.__call__` method. When the domain part fails the `DOMAIN_REGEX` match, it tries to encode it with IDNA encoding. However, the IDNA encoding via Python's `encode("idna")` doesn't validate that labels don't start or end with hyphens - it encodes them successfully even if they're malformed.

The fix is to validate each label of the domain after IDNA encoding to ensure no label starts or ends with a hyphen.
